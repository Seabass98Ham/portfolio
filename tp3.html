TP3 • HEC Montréal

As part of our MSc coursework at HEC Montréal, we conducted a full-cycle UX design project focused on translating user research into actionable design solutions. The project began with in-depth research, where we identified key user needs and pain points through interviews and card sorting. Building on these insights, we developed a design system and iterated on low-fidelity prototypes to rapidly explore concepts and validate core user flows. The project culminated in a presentation showcasing the end-to-end design process.
