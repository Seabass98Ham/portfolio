About me

I'm a communications specialist and visual storyteller. I hold a BA in Psychology from McGill University and am currently pursuing an MSc in Marketing at HEC Montréal. I take a minimalist, modern, and user-centred approach to everything I create, prioritizing impactful and transparent content. Ethics and sustainability are central to my perspective, too, shaping all aspects of my life and the stories I help tell. Outside of my creative work, I'm an avid soccer player and a proud member of the Gugus, one of McGill's longest-standing soccer clubs.
